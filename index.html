Vehicles

Most providers offer two types of vehicles: Full-Size E-cycles and E-Scooters.

Full Size E-Cycles Book now

Most full size e-cycles have a step-in design that allows a user to gracefully seat oneself on the cycle. E-cycle have a motor placed centrally, behind the pedal fulcrum. Like a regular cycle, an E-Cycle still has a range of gears and you'll only recieve assistance while you're pedalling. This gives excellent hill climbing ability. Furthermore, E-Cycles also has a basket to help you carry your heaviest of loads. 21 inch rim with wide tyres ensure they can be used safely in puddles and rain.
Range: 50 miles. Max Speed: Limited to 20mph. Acceration: 2 seconds (0-20mph).

With wider base, the new e-scooters provide more stability, more power and more range. With broad 12 inch tyres, they can be used in puddles and rain. They 700W motor gives excellent hill climibing ability. They also have an adjustable handle bar should you need it to be High/ Medium or Low. T

Benefits

Use one of our E-Cycles spread across London for your next ride/adventure.

Why choose Electric Cycles:

  • Arrive earlier: Average user takes nearly 1 hour to commute to work. Get on a cycle and cut journey times by half

  • Safer journey: With dedicated cycle lanes commuting in busy inner cities is not just faster, but also safer

  • Greener planet: With zero emmissions, take care of our planet and future generations by riding cycles

  • More affordable: A monthly pass costs just GBP 60, less than half the cost of a zone 1-3 tube pass

  • Not just quicker, rental services offered by providers such as Lime, Tide and others is cheaper than a typical day fare on bus and tube combined
